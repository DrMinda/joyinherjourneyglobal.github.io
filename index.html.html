Meet Dr. Minda Reed

Dr. Minda is a physician, public health leader, strategist, speaker, and transformational coach who helps high-achieving women pivot with power, not panic. She created Joy in Her Journey for the woman who is accomplished on paper but exhausted in real life, the one who has spent years proving, performing, and pushing through, and now knows deep down she is meant for more. More alignment. More peace. More impact. More joy.

What makes Dr. Minda different is that she does not coach from theory alone. She coaches from leadership, from lived experience, and from a deep understanding of what it means to navigate high-pressure spaces while carrying extraordinary responsibility. She brings a rare blend of analytical rigor and emotional intelligence to her coaching, and her true gift is her ability to see the woman behind the accomplishments.

Her clients are brilliant, capable, and dependable women who are quietly carrying burnout, disappointment, and the weight of unrealized desire. They may be successful and still feel invisible. They may be doing meaningful work and still feel misaligned. Dr. Minda helps them navigate that space with clarity, courage, and strategy, because your next chapter should not be built from survival mode. It should be built with power.
