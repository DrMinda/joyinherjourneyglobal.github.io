The Truth Nobody Is Saying

You keep telling yourself you'll rest once things calm down. They never calm down.

You are not lazy. You are not unmotivated. You are operating on empty and calling it normal because everyone around you is doing the same thing.

Rest has been reframed as a reward you have not earned yet instead of the strategy that makes everything else possible.

On Wednesday, we are reframing rest entirely, as the pivot itself, not the thing you do after it.
